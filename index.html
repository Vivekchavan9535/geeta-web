येऽप्यन्यदेवता भक्ता यजन्ते श्रद्धयान्विता: | तेऽपि मामेव कौन्तेय यजन्त्यविधिपूर्वकम् || 23||


Swami Vivekananda Says — Later on we read what Krishna says,
“Even those who worship other deities are really worshipping me.”
It is God incarnate whom man is worshipping.
Would God be angry if you called Him by the wrong name? He would be no God at all! Can’t you understand that whatever a man has in his own heart is God — even if he worships a stone? What of that![Source]


Question: How should man worship God?

Answer: With faith.
